Make Your Own Fairytale

Welcome to a magical journey for all ages! Please be our guest for enchanting dinners, magical cabins, beautiful ball gowns, and more! There's always something to do at Once Upon; and always something magical to discover.

Explore Our Themed Rooms

Castle

Perfect for princesses and knights, or kings and queens, our castle suites feature grand king beds, as well as an elegant ottoman bed, leather pullout couch, and twin carriage bed. Admire the rooms hidden crowns and jewels, and bask in the glory of the royal bathtub, while soaking up the provided golden bubbles.

Cabin

Perhaps you prefer the story of a fairy, or living in a house with seven dwarves. Our cabin features both a rustic king size bed, as well as magically carved bunk beds. At night, cozy up by the fire and read from the witches book of spells. Or, if you prefer, search your room for mischievous fairies.

Ammenities

Explore

Once Upon expands far beyond our humble castle walls- set on 5 acres, there's plenty of outdoor magic going on too! Explore the magical forest, stopping for a visit at Cinderella's willow tree or Snow Whites wishing well. Continue on to the royal garden, or get lost in the White Rabbit's hedge maze. There is always magic and adventure at every turn!

Imagine

Here, everyone is encouraged to embrace their inner fairytale. At Once Upon, you don't have to be you! We have costumes for all shapes, sizes, and ages to help leave the real world behind when you step foot into once upon. While here, take on the role of a doting princess, a mischievous fairy, an evil witch, or anything else your creative mind can come up with!

Play

Playtime isn't just for kids! While little ones can enjoy storybook characters and playscapes, couples and parents can treat themselves to a magical photoshoot for two, or a trip to our Fairy God Mother spa! Teens can enjoy the ballroom dance party, shopping, or lazing around in Snow White's Cabin lounge. To wrap up your night with some family time, consider a trip to the Fairy Pool or some light ballroom dancing.

Eat

Pied Piper

The Pied Piper has quick-service Italian down to a science! Drop in for pizza and pasta, accompanied by desserts made and delivered by the fairy god mothers!

Emerald City Eats

Step into the merry old land of Oz! Start off with a wicker basket of rolls, before moving onto a yellow brick soup or a Kansas City steak. Be sure to stay around to say hello to the flying monkeys!

Fae's Foods

Fae's is our in-hotel high end restaurant. Open late so couples can share a bottle of wine after a long day of vacationing. Be sure to drop in for a romantic fairytale dinner.
